"Visions Beyond The Imaginable"

An Indie Game Development Studio focused on Visual Novels.

The Emperor's Fate is a Ren'Py visual novel combining historical fiction and modern mystery. Xu Hua, a top history student, discovers an ancient book in the library that transports her back to the Xu Dynasty. In her dreams as Wu Jinghua, she faces palace intrigue and becomes determined to uncover the truth behind her time-traveling experiences with the help of her friend Fan Li Jun, a history enthusiast.

A Simulator for Electronic Circuits Utilizing Augmented Reality.

This includes two main features: a circuit simulation function and a components recognition tool.

The circuit simulation feature offers the user the ability to craft circuits using their mobile device's Augmented Reality capabilities.

The component recognition tool, on the other hand, incorporates ChatGPT. This allows the user to pinpoint various components, and also to get explanations and understand the particulars of each component with the help of GPT.
