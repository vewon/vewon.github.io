"Visions Beyond The Imaginable"

An Indie Game Development Studio focused on Visual Novels.

The Emperor's Fate is a Ren'Py visual novel combining historical fiction and modern mystery. Xu Hua, a top history student, discovers an ancient book in the library that transports her back to the Xu Dynasty. In her dreams as Wu Jinghua, she faces palace intrigue and becomes determined to uncover the truth behind her time-traveling experiences with the help of her friend Fan Li Jun, a history enthusiast.
